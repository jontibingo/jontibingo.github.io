OUR STORY

Mr Pastry is a product development company based in Corsham, Wiltshire.


We are Steve and Lyn Piercy and we make concept products from scratch, to a given brief, using only the very best ingredients.


Between us we have experience of working in British and French bakeries, top notch restaurants and cafes. We have significant experience in scale up, staff training, and Capex.
